How it Works

Create a workout of favorite exercises and assign them a value.

50 Sit Ups
25 Push Ups

You start with a balance of 0 points. Every night, we deduct 100.

Do one of your exercises and log it to earn points against your balance.

If you keep a positive balance, you're on a streak. Fall below 0 and you break it.

Every night we'll send you an email to remind you.
